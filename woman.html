The Story

Polar Siam began as a question: what does a body wear when it lives in two worlds at once?

Cut from technical cold-weather membranes and finished with the drape of tropical tailoring, every piece is built to be worn against itself. We design for the in-between — the airport at 4am, the rooftop at dusk, the cold open and the warm close.

Made in small runs. Numbered. Never restocked.
